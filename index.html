Cristal Collector

How to play Cristal Collectors

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. You will see 4 cristals bellow. By clicking on a cristal you will be added a specific ammount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score is passes that number.The value of each cristal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts the game will change the value of the cristals.
